Ambulatory Travel & Dispatch Policies

We work closely with farm owners and stable coordinators to make clinical veterinary services affordable and highly coordinated. By planning routine visits, travel fees can be minimized.

Schedule a Shared Stable Call

🤝 Shared Farm Call Program

Coordinate checks with your neighbors! If 2 or more clients book appointments at the same facility or neighboring properties (within 2 miles) on the same day, we split the travel fee equally. For 4+ clients, the travel fee is entirely waived.

🚛 Extended Zone Consultations

Farms located outside our standard 50-mile radius can request consultations for surgeries, herd programs, or equine pre-purchase exams. Travel routes for these visits are pre-scheduled once per month to spread travel expenses among multiple clients.

Biosecurity & Cleanliness Standards

Our ambulatory units operate under strict clinical sanitation procedures to prevent cross-contamination and safeguard herd health.

Boot Disinfection

Veterinarians step through fresh Virkon-S broad-spectrum disinfectant chemical boot baths immediately upon arriving and leaving every property.

Rig Sanitation

Vehicle tires, wheel wells, and chassis parts are sprayed with virucidal solutions between farm zones to prevent regional pathogens from travelling.

Sterilized Instruments

All clinical tools, diagnostic probes, and surgical instruments undergo complete autoclaving sterilization cycles on-board or back at the main base clinic.

Protective Apparel

Our practitioners wear disposable surgical gowns, aprons, and examination gloves for any suspect or infectious cases, disposing of waste safely off-farm.
