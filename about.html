About FarmVet Care

Dedicated to the health, performance, and well-being of large animals for over 15 years.

Our Mission & Values

At FarmVet Care, our mission is to provide gold-standard, compassionate veterinary care to the equine and livestock communities. We value integrity, continuous education, and building lasting relationships with farm owners and stable managers.

The Pillars of Our Practice

Our daily clinical work is anchored by four fundamental values dedicated to large animal care.

Clinical Excellence

Equipping our veterinary trucks with state-of-the-art diagnostic gear to enable hospital-grade on-site treatments.

On-Site Convenience

Bringing advanced diagnostics and minor surgeries straight to your stables, eliminating transport stress.

Long-Term Partnership

Providing routine checks and continuous communication to maximize the lifetime longevity of your herd.

Continuous Education

Actively training our practitioners in advanced equine therapies and bovine health protocols.

Our Journey & Milestones

Trace the growth of FarmVet Care from a single-truck practice into the region's premier mobile clinic.

2011

Company Foundation

Dr. Sarah Jenkins launches FarmVet Care with a single custom-outfitted pickup truck to service local equine properties within a 20-mile radius.

2016

Route System Expansion

We expand our service radius to 50 miles, adding a second specialized truck and introducing dedicated Northern and Southern service route days.

2021

Digital Diagnostics Integration

Upgrade our fleet with portable high-definition digital radiology (X-ray), soft-tissue ultrasound systems, and on-board chemistry lab analyzers.

Our Mobile Ambulatory Fleet

To provide high-quality care without transporting stressed animals, we operate a fleet of customized four-wheel-drive mobile veterinary trucks.

Each unit functions as a self-contained clinic, prepped for both routine calls and emergency trauma treatments.

Automated Medical Refrigeration

Maintains precise temperatures for vaccines, diagnostics, and heat-sensitive therapeutics.

On-Board Auxiliary Power

Fitted with generator backups to run power floats, digital imaging computers, and analyzers.

Real-Time Portal Database Sync

Automatic synchronization to update digital client records immediately during farm visits.

Community & Agricultural Outreach

We believe in supporting the next generation of farmers and educating stable managers on basic first aid.

Youth Livestock Support

We provide complimentary veterinary consults and support for local 4-H clubs and FFA (Future Farmers of America) youth livestock projects at county fairs.

First Aid Workshops

We host regular spring seminars teaching horse owners and barn managers how to measure vitals (pulse, respiration, temperature) and triage colic signs.
