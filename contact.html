Is This a Medical Emergency?

If your animal is showing active signs of colic, injury, or severe distress, please follow these steps immediately:

📞 1. Dial 1-800-VET-FARM

Call our emergency dispatch line immediately. Do not submit an online form for emergencies, as forms are checked next business day.

⚠️ 2. Keep the Animal Stable

Move the animal to a secure box stall or dry corral. Halter them and do not allow colicky animals to roll violently.

💊 3. Hold Medications

Do not administer anti-inflammatories (such as Banamine) or other drugs until paged back by our on-call veterinarian.
